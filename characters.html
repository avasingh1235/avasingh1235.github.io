Meet some of the most well-known witches and wizards from the Harry Potter series. Each plays an important role in shaping the story.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter, known as the Boy Who Lived, is the main protagonist of the story, as he survived a lethal curse from Lord Voldemort, who killed his parents. Harry learns of his magical heritage on his eleventh birthday, and then begins to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger, the brightest witch in her year at Hogwarts, is one of Harry Potter's best friends. She is intelligent, and comes from two Muggle (non-magic) parents. Hermione is also in Gryffindor House.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is Harry Potter's loyal best friend, known for his humor. He is also in Gryffindor House.

Albus Dumbledore

Headmaster of Hogwarts, Albus Dumbledore is one of the most well-known and powerful wizards. He is the only wizard Lord Voldemort fears, and he is wise, kind, and very stratgic. In the later movies, he takes Harry under his wing to help him on quests to kill Lord Voldemort once and for all.

Professor McGonagall

Professor McGonagall is the Transfiguration professor at Hogwarts and head of Gryffindor House. She is very disciplined and strict, but has a clear soft spot for Harry and his friends.

Rubeus Hagrid

Rubeus Hagrid is a gentle half-giant who takes care of the magical creatures on the property and serves as Hogwart's Keeper of Keys and Grounds. He has a close relationship with Harry, Ron, and Hermione.

Draco Malfoy

Draco Malfoy is Harry's rival and a major antagonist for the series. He is a member of the Slytherin House and becomes a Death Eater in his teenage years, aiding Voldemort. He eventually deflects from this.

Professor Severus Snape

Potions Master and head of Slytherin House at Hogwarts, Professor Severus Snape is incredibely hostile towards Harry Potter in his early years at Hogwarts. While initially a central antagonist and affiliated with Lord Voldemort, the mysterious Snape is later revealed to be a double agent working for the Order of the Phoenix, which are the good guys fighting against Lord Voldemort.

Lord Voldemort

Lord Voldemort is the Dark Lord who seeks power and immortality. The series revolves around his attempts to kill Harry, and Harrys attempts to do the same to him. Lord Voldemort seeks to conquer the Wizarding World, exterminate Muggle presence, and he gave Harry his iconic scar.
