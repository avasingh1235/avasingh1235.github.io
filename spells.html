Here are some well-known spells from the Harry Potter universe. Make sure to practice wand safety and proper pronunciation!

Easy + Quick Spells To Know

These are spells that are beginner level.

  • Lumos - This spell creates light at the end of your wand. Very helpful in times of darkness!
  • Nox – This spell extinguishes that wand light.
  • Alohamora – This spell quickly unlocks doors.
  • Wingardium Leviosa – This spell allows objects to levitate.
  • Accio – This spell summons objects.

More Advanced Spells

These are spells that are a bit more advanced.

  • Expelliarmus - This is a disarming charm that can knock your opponent's wand right out of their hands.
  • Protego - This is a shield charm that blocks hexes and jinxes.
  • Stupefy - This is a stun spell that temporarily knocks out your target, leaving them unconscious.
  • Petrificus Totalus - This is the Full-Body Bind Curse that paralyzes your target in place, yet they are still conscious!
  • Reparo - This charm repairs broken objects by mending.
  • Expecto Patronum - This spell is advanced, and is used as a defense against Dementors by conjuring a Patronus to repel them.
