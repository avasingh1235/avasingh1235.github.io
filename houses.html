Students at Hogwarts are sorted into four houses by the sentient Sorting Hat. Each first-year student wears the Sorting Hat during the annual Start-of-Term Feast ceremony. Each house values different traits.

Gryffindor

  • Traits: Courage and Bravery
  • Colors: Red and Gold
Founder:
- Godric Gryffindor
Mascot:
- Lion

Hufflepuff

  • Traits: Loyalty and Patience
  • Colors: Yellow and Black
Founder:
- Helga Hufflepuff
Mascot:
- Badger

Ravenclaw

  • Traits: Intelligence and Wit
  • Colors: Blue and Bronze
Founder:
- Rowena Ravenclaw
Mascot:
- Eagle

Slytherin

  • Traits: Cunning and Ambitious
  • Colors: Green and Silver
Founder:
- Salazar Slytherin
Mascot:
- Serpent
