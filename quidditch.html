Quidditch is the most popular sport within the Wizarding World. Players ride on flying broomsticks and race to score the most points. Each house within Hogwarts has its own Quidditch team, with huge matches that the whole school attends.

How the Game Works

  • Each team has seven players in the air at a time.
  • Three of these players are Chasers, who try to score points by hitting the Quaffle through the opposing team's three hoops
  • Two of the players are Beaters, who defend their teemates from Bludgers.
  • The remaining player is the Seeker, who spends the whole game looking for Golden Snitch.

Famous Quidditch Terms

  • Quaffle: The primary object in a game of Quidditch, the Quaffle is a leather scoring ball.
  • Bludger: An enchanted ball that aims to knock opposing players off of their brooms.
  • Golden Snitch: A small, shiny, golden, winged ball that flutters about the stadium, that is worth 150 points and thus ends the game if caught by the winning team.
